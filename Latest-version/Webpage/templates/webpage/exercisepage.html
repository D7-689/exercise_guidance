Overhead Press

Procedure
  • Stand with the bar on your front shoulders, and your hands next to your shoulders

  • Press the bar over your head, until it’s balanced over your shoulders and mid-foot

  • Lock your elbows at the top, and shrug your shoulders to the ceiling


Barbell Straight Leg Deadlift

Procedure
  • Stand with your mid-foot under the barbell

  • Bend over and grab the bar with a shoulder-width grip

  • Bend your knees until your shins touch the bar

  • Lift your chest up and straighten your lower back

  • Take a big breath, hold it, and stand up with the weight

BARBELL ROW

Procedure
  • Stand with your mid-foot under the bar (medium stance)

  • Bend over and grab the bar (palms down, medium-grip)

  • Unlock your knees while keeping your hips high

  • Lift your chest and straighten your back

  • Pull the bar against your lower chest

SQUAT

Procedure
  • Stand with the bar on your upper-back, and your feet shoulder-width apart

  • Squat down by pushing your knees to the side while moving hips back

  • Break parallel by Squatting down until your hips are lower than your knees

  • Squat back up while keeping your knees out and chest up

  • Stand with your hips and knees locked at the top
